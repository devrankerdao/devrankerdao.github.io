WHO WE ARE

Within the DAO, we have Guild of Gamers who congregate around our gaming sub-guild, GameRanker.GG. Who truly understands the desire of each and every video game players from every generations to P2E.

More than that, we want to empower our guild members to get the full benefits of their membership of the guild through innovative concepts taken through the emergent socialfi ecosystem.
